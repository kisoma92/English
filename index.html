Introduction of grammar

GRAMMARS

What is Grammar

Grammar is the set of rule that explain how the words are used in language through both writing and speaking Or is the system of rules that allow us to structure sentence during writing and speaking

Why We must learn Grammar?

It help to write and speak English in right way even to structure sentence.Also it includes several aspects of the English language like Parts of Speech

Parts of speech

There are Eight(8) basic parts of speech.These are
  1. Noun
  2. Pronoun
  3. Adverb
  4. Adjective
  5. Preposion
  6. conjunction
  7. Article

    8. Let us start with nouns

    Nouns

    what is the nouns?

    Nouns are words we use for something,people and animals and places,as well as for thing like ideas,feeling,and thoughts

    Example of Nouns

    • Animal nouns-Cat,Cow and
    • People nouns-Amina Juma,Frank and Ally
    • Feelings nouns-Love and pain,
    • Things nouns-House,book,pen stone
    We can usually ask What? or Who?When we want to find out which word in the sentence is noun

    For example

    Ally had an Ideas->Who had an Ideas? What did Ally have?an Idea There are two nouns in this sentence.Ally and Idea are nouns

    Types of nouns

    There are two main types of Noun.These are
    1. Proper noun
    2. Common nouns

    1. Proper noun

      2. These are nouns are the names of specific things,place,or people,Example of proper noun are Tanzania,Mr.mwasi,Dodoma and English.Proper nouns are spelled with capital first letter.Your name is proper name

    2. Common noun

      3. Common noun Don't have a capital first letter, except at the beginning of a sentence.

      Example of Common nouns

      book,rain,fork,music and summer

      Now let as see how to change singular into plural nouns

      When we use a noun we have to show if there is one of them(singular) or plural.Most of nouns Simply have -s at the end to show plural.

      For example
      Noun singular plural
      book book books
      Flower flower flowers
      boy boy boys
      girl girl girls
      interest interest interests

      But there are some exception s you must Note

      Note down the following

      Note 1

      All singular nouns that end on an -s,-sh,-ch,-z or -x==> add -es

      For example
      Noun singular plural
      box box boxes
      wish wish wishes
      buzz buzz buzzes
      bus bus buses

      Note2

      Singular noun ending on consonant+y .Take away -y and add -ies for plural

      Noun singular plural
      monkey monkey monkeys
