What differences between countable and uncountable

Countable nouns have a plural form but uncountable noun don't

Example of countable nouns

  • One apple
  • Two apples

Example of uncountable nouns

  • Lump of sugar
  • Two spoonfuls of sugar

We can use a or an before a countable nouns but not before uncountable nouns

For example

a bean,An apple but not sugar, sunlight
