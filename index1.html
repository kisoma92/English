Pronouns

what is pronouns?

Pronounsare words that we use in place of noun

What are types of pronouns

There are three main types of pronoun these are:

  1. personal pronouns
  2. Relative pronoun
  3. possessive pronouns

    4. Let us start with Personal pronouns

    personal pronouns can be divided in two groups

    1. Personal Singular pronoun(he,she,it)
    2. Personal plural pronoun(you,we ,they)
